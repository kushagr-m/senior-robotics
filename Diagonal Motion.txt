power = Clockwise spin
-1*power = Counter-Clockwise spin

Move Forward Right:
FL(-1*power)
FR(0)
BL(0)
BR(power)

Move Forward Left:
FL(0)
FR(power)
BL(-1*power)
BR(0)

Move Backwards Right:
FL(0)
FR(-1*power)
BL(power)
BR(0)

Move Backwards Left:
FL(power)
FR(0)
BL(0)
BR(-1*power)
