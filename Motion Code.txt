Let P be the power from 0% to 100%
P = Clockwise
-P = Counter-Counter

Moving Forward:			Power:
Front right wheel: 		P*0.5*root(2)
Front Left wheel: 		-P*0.5*root(2)
Back right wheel: 		P*0.5*root(2)
Back Left wheel: 		-P*0.5*root(2)

Moving Backward:
Front right wheel: 		-P*0.5*root(2)
Front Left wheel: 		P*0.5*root(2)
Back right wheel: 		-P*0.5*root(2)
Back Left wheel: 		P*0.5*root(2)

Strafing Right:
Front right wheel: 		-P*0.5*root(2)
Front Left wheel: 		-P*0.5*root(2)
Back right wheel: 		P*0.5*root(2)
Back Left wheel: 		P*0.5*root(2)

Strafing Left:
Front right wheel: 		P*0.5*root(2)
Front Left wheel: 		P*0.5*root(2)
Back right wheel: 		-P*0.5*root(2)
Back Left wheel: 		-P*0.5*root(2)

Rotate Clockwise:
Front right wheel: 		-P
Front Left wheel: 		-P
Back right wheel: 		-P
Back Left wheel: 		-P

Rotate Counter-clockwise:
Front right wheel: 		P
Front Left wheel: 		P
Back right wheel: 		P
Back Left wheel: 		P


Rotating Clockwise Around Center of Ball:
Front right wheel: 		0
Front Left wheel: 		0
Back right wheel: 		-P
Back Left wheel: 		-P

Rotating Counter-Clockwise Around Center of Ball:
Front right wheel: 		0
Front Left wheel: 		0
Back right wheel: 		P
Back Left wheel:		P
