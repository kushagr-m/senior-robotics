Let power be the power from 0% to 100%
power = Clockwise
-power = Counter-Counter

Moving Forward:			
Front right wheel: 		power*0.5*root(2)
Front Left wheel: 		-power*0.5*root(2)
Back right wheel: 		power*0.5*root(2)
Back Left wheel: 		-power*0.5*root(2)

Moving Backward:
Front right wheel: 		-power*0.5*root(2)
Front Left wheel: 		power*0.5*root(2)
Back right wheel: 		-power*0.5*root(2)
Back Left wheel: 		power*0.5*root(2)

Strafing Right:
Front right wheel: 		-power*0.5*root(2)
Front Left wheel: 		-power*0.5*root(2)
Back right wheel: 		power*0.5*root(2)
Back Left wheel: 		power*0.5*root(2)

Strafing Left:
Front right wheel: 		power*0.5*root(2)
Front Left wheel: 		power*0.5*root(2)
Back right wheel: 		-power*0.5*root(2)
Back Left wheel: 		-power*0.5*root(2)

Rotate Clockwise:
Front right wheel: 		-power
Front Left wheel: 		-power
Back right wheel: 		-power
Back Left wheel: 		-power

Rotate Counter-clockwise:
Front right wheel: 		power
Front Left wheel: 		power
Back right wheel: 		power
Back Left wheel: 		power


Rotating Clockwise Around Center of Ball:
Front right wheel: 		0
Front Left wheel: 		0
Back right wheel: 		-power
Back Left wheel: 		-power

Rotating Counter-Clockwise Around Center of Ball:
Front right wheel: 		0
Front Left wheel: 		0
Back right wheel: 		power
Back Left wheel:		power
